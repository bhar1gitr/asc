About Us

Established in 1983 by Mr. Vikas Gokhale, Associated Consultants has evolved into one of India’s most trusted and respected structural consultancy firms.

With over 3500+ successfully completed projects — from bungalows, townships, commercial complexes, multiplexes, and industrial setups — we deliver end-to-end structural design and detailing services tailored to every project's unique requirements.

Our team consists of highly experienced engineers proficient in global design codes and international standards. Through a combination of innovation, precision, and modern engineering tools, we ensure structural designs that are safe, cost-efficient, and future-ready.

At Associated Consultants, client satisfaction is our core strength. We deliver solutions that balance safety, aesthetics, and functionality, ensuring long-term value for every project.
