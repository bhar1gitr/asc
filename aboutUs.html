About Associated Consultants

Associated Consultants is a leading structural consultancy firm started by Mr. Vikas Gokhale in 1983. With more than 40 years of experience in the construction industry, we have grown into a national multi-disciplinary organization. We have successfully completed more than 3500+ jobs including residential, commercial, multiplexes, and industrial buildings with reputed Builders and Architects.

We primarily focus on all structural design solutions and building design services, including concrete and steel detailing services for all types of high-rise and low-rise residential and commercial buildings. We are well known for perfection and provide economic and efficient solutions which are safe and conform to accepted standards.

Our highly skilled team comprises professional structural engineers and designers, familiar with international steel and concrete design and detailing standards and codes. Due to the versatility of our engineering teams, we are able to offer a wide range of services. Our team tailors client-specific solutions, using cutting-edge technology and innovative methods which can lead to cost and programme savings for our clients.

Our strength is our Client satisfaction. We strive to exceed client requirements by providing them with unique and valued solutions. We utilize our experience and awareness of market design trends to encourage extremely effective designs and provide guidance and foresight to our clients.

"Associated Consultants is an answer for all structural engineering & design demands. We have the capability of handling all types of projects, be it a dream Bungalow or huge township Projects; our services are always provided in a timely fashion with unpatrolled solutions. We take an enthusiastic initiative in all of our projects & strive for nothing less than excellence."
