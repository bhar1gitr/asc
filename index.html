Meet Our Leader

Mr. Vikas Gokhale

Founder & Principal Consultant

A 1979 VJTI graduate who stood 1st in Bombay University in "Structural Analysis & Design", Mr. Gokhale established Associated Consultants in 1983. With over 40 years of experience, he has grown the firm into a national multi-disciplinary organization.

He actively shares his expertise through seminars on Seismic Design of Structures and Project Management Consultancy. Beyond new structures, he is heavily involved in the challenging field of restoration of dilapidated buildings.

Apart from his academic and professional career, he is deeply committed to the community and is attached with many social, spiritual, and religious organizations.
